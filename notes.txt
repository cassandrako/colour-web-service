web-service:

what will it do?
    it will give you a palette with 10 colours based on your favorite colour.

what npm package will it use?
    it will use the palletey npm package.
        https://www.npmjs.com/package/palettey

query: what is your favorite colour?
url parameter: localhost:3001/colors/{color}
post data: based on your favorite colour, here is 10 complementary colours that could help you build an outfit/design a poster.
